About Us And Our Project

What is This Project?

Genquality is a website built as a project for September's Hackathon with Code Institute, Deloitte and Trust in Soda. The goal of our website is to see how aware the people really are about the gender gap, and provide them with the resources to help make a difference in their own lives and their workplaces.
